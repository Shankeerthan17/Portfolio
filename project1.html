Smart Plant Buddy

Project Info

an IoT-based smart gardening system designed to help users monitor and care for their plants efficiently. It uses sensors to track soil moisture, temperature, and environmental conditions, and provides real-time updates to ensure optimal plant health.


The system can automatically alert users when plants need water or care, making plant maintenance easier, smarter, and more efficient—especially for busy users or beginners in gardening.
